Senda is a digital payment solution designed for people over 70 who need to make online transactions securely and easily. Through a wallet linked to a physical or digital card, it allows users to pay without exposing their main bank account or relying on complex applications, promoting independence and reducing digital exclusion.

Read more

This project arose from identifying the barriers this group faces in digital environments, such as distrust of online payments, dependence on family members, and the loss of social and well-being opportunities. The lack of accessible tools not only limits their autonomy but also their active participation in daily life.

Based on these insights, Senda proposes a system that prioritizes clarity, trust, and ease of use, eliminating the need to interact with traditional banking environments. In this way, it positions itself as a safe and accessible way to pay online without fear of scams, returning control to users.

Its impact translates into the restoration of autonomy for a key group, while generating value for institutions and communities by fostering more active and secure inclusion.
